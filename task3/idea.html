IDEATHON

Ideathon is an initiative to provide students with a platform to identify challenges and provide innovative solutions to some of the important problems we face in our daily lives. It will inculcate a culture of product innovation and a mindset of problem-solving with vast industrial and societal applications. Students are encouraged to submit their ideas to jump-start by sculpting their innovation. Potential solution prototypes will be given with high priority. Interested students are requested to submit their Novel/Innovative ideas for any applications.

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Team size should be 2 to 3 members (Preferably Interdepartmental)
  2. All the ideas should be convertible into real-time solutions through suitable platforms.
  3. Best ideas will be selected and the proposers will be called to present their ideas to the jury panel members.
  4. PPT or PDF file to be submitted
  5. Inclusions - Abstract, Problem definition, Methodology or Implementation
  6. The ideas proposed should be
    • Unique
    • Based on scientific backing
    • To provide solutions to the present-day issues or improve the efficiency of the current system in place.
  7. Last Date for abstract submission: 10th MAY
  8. Entire project to be presented on the day of the event
  9. Payment: 100Rs per team (to be paid before the deadline for abstract submission)
