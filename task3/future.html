Some people argue that better technologies can increase the efficiency of extracting resource deposits from areas where resources have already been depleted. The real sustainable future lies in developing diligent practices in our worldwide use of natural resources including conservation, recycling, and reuse of resources, and in the adoption of sustainable resource management strategies on national and local levels.
