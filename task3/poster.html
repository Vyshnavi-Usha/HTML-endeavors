POSTER MAKING

Technical poster making typically is that you need to prepare and present a poster when your work is not ready for a published paper or an official talk slot. The poster session permits one-on-one (or one-on-few) interaction . The greatest value of the poster is when you are standing in front of it, and you should optimize for that. During other times, it can still convey information to passers-by


INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Team size should be 2 to 3 members (Preferably Interdepartmental)
  2. Initial submission: Submit ONE page write-up. Maximum 300 words.
  3. After acceptance on the day of event the team is expected to present their poster (10 minutes)
  4. Templates for poster will be shared after acceptance.
  5. Last Date for abstract submission: 10th MAY
  6. Entire poster to be presented on the day of the event
  7. Payment: 100Rs per team (to be paid before the deadline for abstract submission)
