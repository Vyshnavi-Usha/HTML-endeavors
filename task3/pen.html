PEN TESTING

Pen testing competition is to help students develop cyber security skills. In the simulated environment, student teams identify problems. Then, they address the cyber security issues in the network. Finally, they deal with any vulnerabilities.


INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Team size should be 2 to 3 members (Preferably Interclass)
  2. LEVEL1: Inter-class
  3. LEVEL2: Inter-department
  4. LEVEL3: Inter-college
  5. Payment: 100Rs per team (to be paid before the date of the event)
